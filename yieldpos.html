Point of Sale Software

Built for fresh retail.

YieldPOS keeps busy checkout lanes moving — touchscreen product keys, barcode scanning, weighed produce, EFTPOS payments, and full back-office control. Runs offline on Windows. No subscription. No fragile cloud dependency.

Book a demo  → See features  ↓
Offline
Runs locally on Windows. Keeps selling when the internet drops.
EFTPOS
Linkly Cloud integration. Card payments built in, no extra hardware.
Scales
Live scale weight for weighed produce and bulk items.
Multi-lane
LAN and cloud sync keeps all registers aligned in real time.
Back Office

Full visibility. Real time.

  • Daily & weekly sales summary
  • Sales by hour, category, and payment method
  • Top-selling products at a glance
  • Product, pricing & category management
  • Deals and specials control
  • Staff PIN and role management
  • Stock tracking and low-stock alerts
  • Transaction search and CSV export
  • Shelf label and price tag printing
  • Z reports and end-of-day reconciliation
Register

Fast lanes. No slowdowns.

  • Staff PIN login with roles
  • Touchscreen product keyboard
  • Barcode and PLU lookup
  • Product search by name or category
  • Held and parked sales with recall
  • Cash tender with change calculation
  • Linkly Cloud EFTPOS integration
  • Returns and refunds
  • Receipt printing and reprint
  • No-sale and open-drawer functions
Fresh Retail

Built for weighed produce.

  • Serial scale integration (Mettler Toledo)
  • Per-kg pricing and weight lookup
  • Open-price weighed products
  • Each and kg unit types per product
  • Produce category pages on touchscreen
  • Product images for fast visual ID
  • Configurable keyboard pages
  • Fruit & veg quick-select buttons

Customer Display

Your customers see every item.

A dedicated customer-facing display runs alongside the register — showing the live basket, running total, payment prompt, and a thank-you screen when the sale is complete. No additional system required.

  • Idle branded display between sales
  • Live basket with items and quantities
  • Running total with GST shown
  • Payment prompt screen
  • Payment approved confirmation
  • Sale complete / thank-you screen

Keyboard Layout

Your products, your layout.

Set up the touchscreen keyboard exactly the way your staff use it. Organise product pages by department, assign produce keys and images, and push the layout to all registers in one step.

  • Unlimited configurable keyboard pages
  • Product images on buttons
  • Category-based page organisation
  • Custom button colours and labels
  • Sync keyboard across all registers instantly
  • Export and import layout files
