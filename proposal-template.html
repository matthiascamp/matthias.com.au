Safeguards & Controls

Every automated action is subject to controls that protect the business and its customers. The system is built to be safe by default — uncertain, unusual, or high-risk items always go to a human.

Control How it works
Staff approval No response or action is sent without a staff member reviewing and confirming it. The system drafts and suggests — people decide.
Sender / customer verification The system cross-references known contacts and account records before acting. Unverified senders are flagged rather than handled automatically.
Minimum access The AI layer only has read access to the systems it needs. It cannot modify orders, pricing, or customer records.
Safe uncertainty When confidence is below a defined threshold, the item is routed to manual review. The system errs toward caution, not assumption.
Pricing & commercial protection Pricing, discounts, and contractual terms are never drafted automatically. Any communication involving commercial commitments requires explicit staff input.
Full logging Every classification, draft, and decision is logged with timestamps and accessible for review. Nothing happens invisibly.
Manual override Staff can override, reject, or escalate any item at any point. The system adapts based on rejection patterns over time.
